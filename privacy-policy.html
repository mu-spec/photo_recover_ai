Privacy Policy

Media Rescue - Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. About This App

Media Rescue helps users find and restore accessible, cached, and recently deleted media (device dependent).

Important: On modern Android devices, true raw deleted-block recovery is generally not possible without root access.

Package: com.photoRecoverAI.photo_recover_ai
Contact: saaddkhan99@gmail.com

2. What We Collect

  • No account, no signup, and no personal profile data.
  • No upload of your photos, videos, or documents to our servers.
  • Scan metadata (name, size, path, modified time) is processed locally on your device.

3. How Scanning Works

Depending on Android version and permissions, the app may scan:

  • Existing media folders (for example DCIM, Pictures, Downloads)
  • Android/media and app-accessible media locations
  • Cache/thumbnail and recycle/trash style paths when available

The app does not guarantee deleted-file recovery on every device.

4. Permissions

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO (Android 13+): read media for scan and preview.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (older Android): media/file access where applicable.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: ad delivery through Google AdMob.

6. Storage and Retention

  • Scan records are stored locally in the app database.
  • Restored copies are saved to /storage/emulated/0/MediaRescue/.
  • Uninstalling the app removes app data; restored copies may remain until you delete them.

7. User Rights

  • You can revoke permissions from Android settings at any time.
  • You can delete scan history and restored copies at any time.
  • You can uninstall the app whenever you choose.
